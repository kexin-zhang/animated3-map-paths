Ira Spring Trail & Gas Works Park

06/23/2018

Hike along Ira Spring Trail to Mason Lake and a rafting trip across Lake Union!

  1. One hour drive to Ira Spring Trailhead
  2. Hike to Mason Lake and back
  3. Drive to Tacos Chukis for food
  4. Drive to parking lot by Lake Union
  5. Raft across Lake Union to Gas Works
  6. Take a Lyft back to the parking lot
